Wonderla Resort, the newest venture of Wonderla Holidays Limited, is a first-of-its-kind resort in India with an AMUSEMENT PARK attached to it. A never before combination of leisure and luxury, Wonderla Resort is built inside India's favourite amusement park - Wonderla, Bangalore. A "luxury" extension to Wonderla amusement park, this resort surrounded by acres of lush greenery, will offer the finest in hospitality. A holiday resort as well as a business hotel, the facilities here include fully equipped business/celebration halls, a swish resto-o-bar 'Red Ice', a fine dining theme restaurant 'Woods', ultra luxury rooms, heated pool and recreation area and of course, an amusement park in its backyard.
